# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We want to make this project welcoming and inclusive.  
As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, submitting pull requests, providing feedback, or any other form of participation.

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or level of experience.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive.  
- Offering constructive feedback.  
- Gracefully accepting differences of opinion.  
- Focusing on what is best for the community.  

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Use of sexualized language or imagery.  
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insults.  
- Public or private harassment.  
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent.  

---

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers have the right to:
- Remove, edit, or reject contributions not aligned with this Code of Conduct.  
- Ban temporarily or permanently anyone who violates these rules.  

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces where someone is representing the project.

---

## Enforcement
If you experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers privately.  
All complaints will be reviewed and addressed fairly.
